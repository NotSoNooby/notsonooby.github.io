Rock Paper Scissors Lizard Spock

Rock Paper Scissors Lizard Spock is a game from the popular TV show The Big Bang Theory. This game takes Rock Paper Scissors and adds 2 new elements, Lizard and Spock. The rules are simple: Rock crushes Scissors and crushes Lizard. Paper covers Rock and disproves Spock. Scissors cuts Paper and decapitates Lizard. Lizard eats Paper and poisons Spock. Spock smashes Scissors and vaporizes Rock.
